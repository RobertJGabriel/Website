About Me

I am an Interaction designer and engineer. I currently work at Teamwork.com. I studied computer science at Cork Institute of Technology in Ireland and graduated at the top of my class. For the last five years, I have been developing and designing several popular web apps and websites, such as the chrome extension, Open Dyslexia, which has 29,000 daily users. In my design process I prioritize accessibility before anything else and as a result, I have won fifteen awards for design interaction, Ux, API design and code design.
